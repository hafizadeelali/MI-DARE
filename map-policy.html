Minimum Advertised Price Policy

Effective March 2023, All MiDARE products will be subject to a Minimum Advertised Price Policy (herein referred to as "MAP Policy").

In this MAP policy, "Dealers" is sellers or value-added resellers that purchase goods from MiDARE Cosmetics, LLC for sale to their end-user customers. This policy is created and agreed by between MiDARE Cosmetics, LLC and Dealers upon purchase.

The purpose of this policy is to protect MiDARE product’s integrity. The MAP Policy guarantees homogeneity, economical balance, and that all products are sold at their proper value. The MAP strategy is also intended to provide resellers with an incentive to invest in MiDARE's customer service.

The Minimum Advertised Price Policy applies to all, but is not limited to, prices advertised on websites, catalogs, in-store displays, flyers, and other forms of media, such as e-commerce and all electronic ways available.

Each product has a predetermined minimum advertised price. All resellers must set their pricing according to the minimum advertised price. Their pricing should be equal to or greater than MiDARE's suggested price.

MiDARE’s minimum advertised price may be updated at any moment and for any amount, when sees fit at its sole discretion. It is the resellers’ responsibility to check MiDARE's website periodically for any adjustments in the MAP Policy.

If a product covered by this MAP Policy is advertised by the Dealers to be free or discounted, lower than the minimum advertised price, the Dealers would be challenging and violating the MAP Policy of the product included in the Minimum Advertised Price Policy.

There is no maximum advertised prices established by this MAP Policy. All resellers can offer MiDARE products at any price as long as it is above the minimum advertised price.
