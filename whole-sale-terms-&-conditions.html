All returns must be made in writing directly to MiDARE Cosmetics, LLC within (14) days of delivery listing both the invoice number and the reason for the return. No returns are permitted without our prior approval and delivery instructions for transport. Shipping costs will be charged. For any goods returned as “unsuitable”, there is a restocking charge of 5% plus shipping chargeable to the customer. Please email us with details of the product before returning it.

MiDARE Cosmetics, LLC does not accept electronic payments at this time due to the cost involved. The order will come either directly from our warehouse or from the manufacturing facility depending on the amount involved.

Shipping is not included in the cost of goods. At checkout, all shipping fees are added and displayed. Shipping rates and times are determined by the weight and size of the individual item(s) and the shipping speed selected.

Please inspect all shipments immediately upon arrival. Please contact MiDARE Cosmetics, LLC at sale@midare-cos.us within seven (7) days of receipt of damaged or defective shipments. Returned merchandise will be replaced, but will not be accepted if it is held for more than fourteen (14) days after receipt.

Please allow at least (3) to (20) business days for your order to arrive, regardless of whether the order is shipped from our warehouse or the manufacturing facility. If the order is shipped from the manufacturing facility, please allow at least (10) extra days for delivery.

Orders are typically processed between one (1) to two (2) working days. On weekends, as well as holidays, orders may not be shipped or fulfilled. Shipments may be delayed due to factors beyond our control, so please allow extra shipping days. We will notify you via email if there is a substantial delay in the dispatch of your parcel.

Outstanding back-orders will be shown on each invoice. Please inform us if you want to cancel any back-order or not have any back-orders listed at all.
